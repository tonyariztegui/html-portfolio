About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Tony, and I currently live in Italy. I have a diverse set of interests and roles. I work as a French tutor, have experience in customer service, and I'm also venturing into the exciting world of web development (fingers crossed for success!).

For almost a decade, I've been involved in customer service, starting from my humble beginnings as a waiter and eventually working my way up to becoming a Team Leader for a multinational company. I genuinely enjoy working in customer service because it allows me to help others, build relationships, and tackle problem-solving challenges. It's truly fulfilling to provide exceptional solutions that meet both customer needs and company goals.

In the past couple of years, I've been working as a French tutor, assisting several secondary schools in Italy with the DELF language certification project at the A2 level. This new experience has quickly become a passion of mine. I love sharing knowledge, seeing my students make progress, and witnessing the positive outcomes of our collaborative efforts.

Looking ahead, I'm excited to pursue another interest of mine: web development. I find the field incredibly fascinating and full of endless possibilities. Additionally, as a fan of video games, I'm eager to explore the realm of building interactive games that not only entertain but also serve as effective tools for language learning.

In summary, I'm an individual driven by a desire to make a positive impact. Whether it's through customer service, teaching French, or delving into web development, I approach each endeavor with enthusiasm and a friendly attitude.
